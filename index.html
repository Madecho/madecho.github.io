I am currently a Lecturer at Jiangxi University of Software Professional Technology. Previously, I obtained my Master’s degree in Computer Science from Nanchang University, where I was advised by Prof. Jingbo Wei. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Software Engineering from Jiangxi University of Finance and Economics.

My research interests lie at the intersection of 3D Vision and AI, specifically focusing on 3D Reconstruction, Point-Cloud Processing, and Generative AI.
